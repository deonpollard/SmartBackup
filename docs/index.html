Frequently Asked Questions

If you haven’t taken deliberate action to back up your SmartSheet data, then it’s not protected. That means you can count on SmartSheet to ensure the security and integrity of the platform, but you are responsible for securing and managing all the data you generate.

  • Do you require SmartBackup to restore a sheet?

    No. All backups are done within Smartsheet, so it is an exact copy of the original in Smartsheet format. Physical restore should be a doddle, of course logically with sheet dependencies (cell-links) may require some consideration.

  • How many times can I use it?

    There is no limit on use, you can do it as frequently as your capacities allow. Of course it make sense to sit down and plan what make sense for your environment. Maybe that means a Full Export once a month with incremental backups every day at 19h00.

  • Can I use it for free?

    Yes the Starter Edition is complete free for life. Understandably it carries retrictions. For full pricing refer to AcuWorkflow

  • Why should I backup my Smartsheet data, surely they cater for that?

    Yes and No, yes they offer a standard backup process which have some limitations. No in a report by Forrester they point out the implications of SaaS platforms and that the User still is responsible for securing their own data. By using SmartBackup we just make it simpler, easier and better.
